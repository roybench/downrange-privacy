## Frequently asked questions

**Does Downrange collect any of my data?**
No. Downrange stores all game progress locally on your device only. No accounts, no analytics, no ads. See our [Privacy Policy](https://roybench.github.io/downrange-privacy/) for full details.

**How do I reset my progress?**
Open Settings within the app and select "Reset Progress."

**The gyroscope aiming feels off — can I adjust it?**
Yes. Go to Settings and adjust the Gyro Sensitivity option to your preference.

**I found a bug. How do I report it?**
Email us at the address above with a description of what happened and, if possible, which device and range/weapon you were using. Screenshots help a lot.

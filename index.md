# Privacy Policy for Downrange

**Last updated: July 13, 2026**

Downrange ("the App") is developed by Roy Raedisch ("we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

## The short version

Downrange does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. All game data stays on your device.

## What data the App stores

The App stores the following information **locally on your device only**, using Apple's SwiftData framework:

- Your in-game rank, lifetime points, and statistics (shots fired, accuracy, best combo, etc.)
- Your weapon unlock and upgrade progress
- A log of your completed shooting sessions (score, weapon used, range, date)
- Your app preferences (volume, control scheme, sensitivity, and similar settings)

None of this information is linked to your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information, because the App never asks for any.

## Data we do not collect

The App does not collect, and has no capability to collect:

- Your name, email address, or contact information
- Your location
- Photos, contacts, or files from your device
- Analytics or usage tracking data
- Advertising identifiers
- Any information transmitted to us or to any third party

## Device sensors

The App uses your device's gyroscope and motion sensors (via Apple's CoreMotion framework) to power the gyroscope aiming mode. This data is processed entirely on your device in real time to control the game's camera and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted anywhere.

## Third-party services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services.

## Data retention and deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you control it directly:

- Use the **Reset Progress** option in the App's Settings to erase all locally stored game data at any time.
- Deleting the App from your device permanently removes all associated data.

## Children's privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13, because it does not collect personal information from any user. The App is rated in accordance with the age rating assigned by the App Store.

## App Store platform data

Apple's App Store and iOS platform may independently collect certain data (such as crash reports or aggregate download statistics) under Apple's own privacy policy, which is separate from this one and outside our control. See Apple's privacy policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/ for details.

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above and post the revised policy at this same address.
